Egyptian Rat Slap

Egyptian Rat Slap is a multiplayer card game where the goal for a player is to gain the entire deck. The deck is split evenly, and players take turns placing cards in the center. There are various ways to gain cards, which can be split into two categories, luck based and skill based. This project was done in C# on Visual Studio with the MonoGame framework.

Features:

  • Minor animation and sound effects
  • Ability to choose the amount of players: 2, 3, or 4
  • Controller and keyboard compatible
