My Service

I am an Eagle Scout, which is the highest rank that can be awarded in the Boy Scouts of America. For my Eagle service project, I organized a food drive for a nonprofit called Mission Oak Cliff. Mission Oak Cliff offers various services, like providing food and clothing, to those living in Dallas who are in need. The first step of this project was to create leaflets that explained who I was, what my project was about, and when and where to leave donations. Next, I gathered some volunteers and we went around my neighborhood and distributed around 200 of the aforementioned leaflets encouraging people to leave food donations on their porches for me to pick up the next week. The next week, my volunteers and I drove around and picked up the donations. I received hundreds of food items and a monetary donation of $100, of which I spent at the grocery store, purchasing items like ready-to-eat food that the charity requested. In the end, I donated over 500 food items to Mission Oak Cliff.

Organizations that I've volunteered with:

  • The Stewpot - A homeless shelter that offers various services, including providing hot meals, providing groceries, providing clothing, assisting in obtaining an ID, and more.
  • For the Love of the Lake - An organization that focuses on the cleanup and preservation of White Rock Lake.
  • Boy Scouts of America (BSA) - I've worked as a volunteer for several other scouts on their Eagle projects.

Organizations that I would like to volunteer with:

  • Girls Who Code - A nonprofit organization that seeks to close the gender gap in computer science by teaching girls, in grades 3-12, how to code.
